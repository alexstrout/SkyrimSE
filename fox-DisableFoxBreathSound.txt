No Idle Fox Breathing Sounds
------
Disables the idle breathing sound on foxes.

[h1][u]Skyrim Special Edition Note[/u][/h1]
[b]This will eventually make its way to SSE - I'll update this section when it does!
As it would not rely on SKSE64, it should be compatible with all platforms.[/b]

[h1]Additional Information[/h1]
Intended to help keep one's sanity when using Kitt or Sola as a pet:
http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=13413

However, this could also just be used on its own if this sound particularly bothers you.

[h1]Uninstall Steps[/h1]
Simply remove the plugin as normal; no save cleaning necessary.
